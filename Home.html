Welcome to CircuitSpark Crates

Welcome to CircuitSpark Crates, your hub for hands-on tech adventures. We create build-it-yourself electronics kits that make learning fun, accessible, and wildly creative. Whether you're a curious kid, a hobbyist adult, or a future engineer, our kits are designed to spark inspiration and give you the power to create. No experience required — just a passion to learn and build.

Each kit comes with everything you need and guides you through real-world concepts in electronics and design. From blinking LEDs to motorized machines, every project builds real skills. Our mission is to make the world of electronics less intimidating and more exciting for everyone. So grab a kit, roll up your sleeves, and let's build something amazing together.

Featured Kits

Build your own clock kit

Build your own fully functional digital clock while learning the fundamentals of circuits, programming, and digital displays. It’s the perfect starting point for anyone curious about how everyday electronics work.

Weather Watch addon for the clock kit

Connect your clock to real-time weather data with this smart internet-enabled add-on. Display current conditions like temperature, humidity, and weather status right on your clock for a futuristic, functional upgrade.

Temperature Tracker addon for the clock kit

This add-on equips your clock with real-time temperature and humidity sensors. Watch your project evolve as it collects and displays live environmental data.

Alarm addon for the clock kit

Add an alarm and custom reminder feature to your clock with this simple yet powerful add-on. Set daily alarms, hourly chimes, or even programmable reminders to stay on track — all controlled through simple buttons.

About Us

CircuitSpark Crates was founded by two high school students with a passion for making electronics fun, hands-on, and accessible. Based in Kingston, Ontario, we design and manufacture educational tech kits that teach real skills through real builds — from clocks to speakers to RC cars. Our goal is to spark curiosity and creativity in people of all ages, whether you're just getting started or diving deeper into how things work. Each kit is carefully designed to be beginner-friendly, customizable, and genuinely exciting to build. With a growing line of products and add-ons, plus the option for custom kits, we’re on a mission to make learning electric.
